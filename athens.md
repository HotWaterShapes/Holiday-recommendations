## Notes
- **Villa Duchess (Penteli) is the best bet** for meeting the 20-min requirement — it's the closest of the three to Athens nightlife.
- For a group wanting beach + Athens nightlife access, a compromise is needed: either accept a 40-min coastal villa or a borderline inland villa.
- All three options are clustered around the same price (~£1,700–1,760).
- Athens nightlife (Gazi, Monastiraki, Psiri) is vibrant and accessible by rideshare.
