## Proximity — ⚠️ DEPENDS ON LOCATION

- **Kavos** is at the far south of Corfu (~45 km from Corfu Town), a self-contained party village. If the villa is IN or near Kavos, it's walking distance to the strip. ✅
- **Corfu Town** has its own nightlife (quieter, more upmarket) in the north. A villa near Corfu Town is ~45 min from Kavos. ❌ for Kavos nightlife.
- "Depiros" suggests a location near Lefkimmi/Kavos area (southern Corfu) — ✅ PASS if correct.
- "Sgouris" — verify exact location. If in the south/Kavos region, passes; if in Corfu Town area, does not.
- **Recommendation:** Confirm villa is in the Kavos / southern Corfu area before booking.

## Notes

- **Kavos is the UK's most notorious party resort** — entirely built around young British tourists. Clubs, bars, pub crawls, and beach parties run all night from late May through September.
- If the group wants a full-on, no-holds-barred party experience, Kavos is the answer.
- At £1,148–£1,373, Corfu is among the cheapest qualifying destinations in the entire search.
- Sgouris at £1,333 for 8BR/10beds is **exceptional value** — roughly £48/person/night including accommodation.
- June is Kavos's opening month: full schedule of club nights and events.
- Good UK charter flights to Corfu Airport (CFU), including from most regional airports.
- Kavos itself has no private pool villas within the strip — pool villas are typically in the surrounding hills/rural areas, which may be 5–10 min drive from Kavos village centre.
