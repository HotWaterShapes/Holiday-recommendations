## ⚠️ Important Location Note

- **Haus Bepo is on Brač island, not Hvar island.** Hvar Town (the party/nightlife hub) is reached by ferry from Brač — roughly a 20–30 min ferry + wait time. This does not meet the "20 min drive" requirement if Hvar Town is the party venue.
- **Villa Roca** and the **Beachfront Infinity Pool Villa** have not had their island verified — check their listings carefully before booking.
- Brač itself (Bol/Golden Horn) is a beautiful beach destination but a quieter alternative to Hvar Town's party scene.

## Notes
- Hvar Town is one of Europe's premier party islands — but ensure the chosen villa is actually on Hvar island.
- If staying on Brač, Bol is excellent for beach days; Hvar Town can be reached by boat as a day/night trip.
- Villa Roca at 5.0★ with 10 beds and the Beachfront Infinity Pool villa are worth verifying for actual Hvar island location.
- June is peak season — book early regardless of island choice.
