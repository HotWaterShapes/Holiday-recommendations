## Proximity — PASS ✅ (if in Kos Town / Kardamena area)

- **Kos Town** (north coast) is the main nightlife hub — bars, clubs, beach bars along the harbour.
- **Kardamena** (south coast) is the dedicated party resort — ~35 km from Kos Town, approximately **40 min drive** to each other.
- Most Kos villas are within 10–20 min of one of these two centres.
- **Verify which area the villa is in** — a villa near Kos Town passes for Kos Town nightlife; a villa near Kardamena passes for Kardamena.

## Notes

- **Kos is a classic UK lads' holiday destination** — Kardamena in particular is known for cheap drinks, foam parties, and a full-on party strip.
- The island is small (290 km²) — even cross-island drives are under 45 min.
- June is ideal: open season, warm (~27°C), not yet August peak.
- Good UK charter flight connections, including from regional airports.
- **Turkey day trip:** Kos is very close to Bodrum (Turkey) — a day trip by ferry is easy and popular.
