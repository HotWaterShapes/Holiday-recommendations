## Notes

- **Benidorm is a well-established British party destination** — the iconic high-rise resort town has clubs (KU Club, Penelope's), bars, and strip pubs. It's more budget/accessible than Ibiza.
- The city has one of the highest concentrations of hotel and villa rentals in Europe.
- June is the start of peak season — clubs are open, beach is active, prices not yet at August peak.
- **Budget-friendly:** Benidorm is significantly cheaper than Ibiza, Mykonos, or even Dubrovnik for food, drinks, and clubs.
- Excellent flight connections — Alicante Airport (ALC) is ~50 km south, ~40 min drive. Many direct UK routes.
- Finestrat villa has a pool and 9 beds across 5BR — everyone gets their own bed with 2 to spare.
