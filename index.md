### Tier 3 — Destination reframe needed
*Good properties, but works only as a different kind of holiday*

- **[Lisbon → Ericeira](lisbon.md)** — Only qualifying villa is in Ericeira surf town, ~40 km from Lisbon nightlife. Great beach holiday, not a Lisbon city-break.
- **[Budapest](budapest.md)** — Horány Villa is 30–40 min from ruin bars. Scenic Danube retreat, but no party proximity. Also no AC.
- **[Hvar / Brač](hvar.md)** — Listed villa is on Brač island, not Hvar — ferry required. Stunning island setting if ferry is acceptable.
- **[Naples → Ischia](naples.md)** — Excellent if treated as an Ischia island holiday. Ferry required.
- **[Gran Canaria](gran_canaria.md)** — All qualifying listings are in Las Palmas (north). Playa del Inglés/Maspalomas (UK resort zone) is 50 km and ~50 min away. Works as a Las Palmas city trip — beaches, bars, culture — but not the UK party resort experience.
- **[Kefalonia](kefalonia.md)** — Scenic island, not a party destination. Best listing is ~25–30 min from Argostoli (modest nightlife). Best framed as a relaxed villa holiday with swimming and food.
