## Notes

- **Skiathos Town** has a lively bar scene, especially in peak season (June–August). It's more of a "beautiful island with good bars" than a dedicated clubbing destination. Parties tend to be outdoor/bar-based rather than big clubs.
- Koukounaries beach (SW coast) is famous — near these villas. Beach bars active in June.
- Skiathos has good summer flight connections from UK airports (Jet2, TUI to Skiathos Airport SKI). Direct flights are seasonal.
- June is ideal: peak season just beginning, warm but not oppressive, fewer crowds than July/August.
- The island is very small — car hire recommended for exploring beyond the immediate area.
