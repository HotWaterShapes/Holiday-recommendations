## Proximity — ⚠️ LAS PALMAS ONLY (not Playa del Inglés)

**Important:** The UK party zone in Gran Canaria is **Playa del Inglés / Maspalomas** (~27.74°N) in the far south. All qualifying listings are in **Las Palmas** (north, ~28.13–28.15°N) or central Gran Canaria — approximately **50 km and 45–55 min** from Playa del Inglés. ❌ FAIL for Playa del Inglés party proximity.

Las Palmas itself has a decent nightlife scene (Playa de las Canteras strip, Vegueta old town bars) — but it's a **city bar scene**, not a UK holiday resort party scene.

- **Las Palmas city bars:** ~10–15 min from the listings ✅ as a city trip.
- **Playa del Inglés clubs:** ~50 min ❌ too far for a base.

## Notes

- Gran Canaria works well as a **Las Palmas city holiday** — beautiful beach (Playa de las Canteras), city restaurants, bars, and culture. Not the same as a Playa del Inglés resort trip.
- The UK party resort (Playa del Inglés) has no qualifying 5BR villas within budget — it's dominated by apartments and hotels. The villa market there is very limited.
- Villa MonteGolf (mid-island, 28.02°N) is ~35 km from Playa del Inglés (~35 min) and ~25 km from Las Palmas (~20 min) — the closest to a compromise, but still fails the proximity requirement.
- June weather: ~25°C, sunny, warm Atlantic, reliably dry.
- Flights to Gran Canaria (LPA) from all major UK airports.
