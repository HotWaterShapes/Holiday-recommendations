## Notes

- **Lanzarote is one of the most popular UK Canary Island destinations** — year-round sunshine, no passport issues, direct flights from UK regional airports (easyJet, Ryanair, Jet2).
- Puerto del Carmen is the main resort — lively bar strip, beach clubs, with nightlife concentrated along the Avenida de las Playas. Lower key than Ayia Napa or Ibiza but a solid lads' choice.
- June is peak season — excellent weather (~27°C), warm Atlantic, quiet on the volcanic side.
- Villa Luciano at £1,196 (4 nights) = ~£43/person/night — extraordinary value for a 5BR villa.
- Lanzarote airport (ACE) has excellent UK connections.
