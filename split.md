## Notes
- **Split is Croatia's second city** — the Diocletian's Palace Old Town is UNESCO-listed and the city has a great bar/club scene along the Riva waterfront.
- Omiš (near VILLA RELAX) is a small town in its own right with summer nightlife, beach, and the Cetina river gorge — a good destination even without Split.
- The Split Dalmatia area is excellent for island hopping — Hvar, Brač, and Vis are all accessible by ferry from Split.
- None of the 5BR+ listings found are within a strict 20 min of Split city centre — Split's urban density means large villas are in surrounding towns.
- **Strongly recommended destination** despite the proximity caveat — the scenery, beaches, and culture are among the best on the Adriatic.
