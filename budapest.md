## Notes

- **Budapest has one of Europe's best party scenes** — the ruin bar scene is unique, lively, and cheap by Western European standards.
- Also known for thermal bath parties (Széchenyi bath parties run on weekends).
- June Budapest weather: ~25°C, pleasant evenings — ideal.
- **Pool villas in Budapest** are rare; most 5BR+ pool properties are in the Buda Hills or outer suburbs (20–30 min from central Pest nightlife).
- The Art Nouveau Villa at £3,361 is the priciest Budapest option — verify it has a pool and confirm proximity to the city before booking.
- Horány is a peaceful Danube retreat — beautiful but not a party base. Better suited to a relaxing holiday than a nightlife-focused trip.
- Hungary is in the EU; no currency conversion issues for Euro holders, but Hungary still uses the Forint (HUF).
- Budget airline connections (Ryanair, Wizz Air) to Budapest Airport (BUD) from UK regional airports are excellent.
