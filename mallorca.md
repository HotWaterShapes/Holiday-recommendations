## Notes
- Mallorca is one of Spain's best villa destinations — huge inventory, beautiful beaches (Es Trenc, Cala d'Or, Alcúdia), excellent food.
- Most 5BR rural fincas on Mallorca are in the interior or north of the island, well away from Palma nightlife.
- **If the party is in Palma:** stick to Playa de Palma / Arenal area villas.
- **If the party is elsewhere on the island** (Port d'Alcúdia, Pollença): northern villas like Can Xeliu become viable.
- Mallorca is very well-served by Airbnb — more searching will reveal better options. The search above returned a wide island-wide result; try searching specifically for "Palma de Mallorca" or "Playa de Palma" for city-adjacent properties.
