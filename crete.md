## Proximity — PASS ✅

- **Hersonissos** and **Malia** are adjacent resort towns on Crete's north coast (~5 km apart, ~8 min drive). Both are major party destinations.
- Villas in or near Hersonissos are walking distance or <10 min to the strip.
- Heraklion (Crete's capital) is ~25 km west of Hersonissos — a separate nightlife scene, but the main reason to visit Crete as a group is Hersonissos/Malia.
- Dievandi Seaview: if the "seaview" suggests a hillside location, it may be 5–10 min above Hersonissos — still a PASS.

## Notes

- **Malia and Hersonissos are Crete's equivalent of Faliraki or Kavos** — purpose-built UK/Irish party resort strips with clubs, foam parties, and beach bars running all night.
- June is good: open season but not yet August peak prices or crowds.
- Crete is the largest Greek island — well-served by charter and scheduled flights from UK airports.
- The island also offers serious cultural/scenic assets (Knossos Palace, Samaria Gorge, Elafonisi beach) if the group wants day trips.
- Dievandi's 4.98★ with 52 reviews is one of the most reliable ratings in the entire search — strongly recommended if in budget.
- At £2,132 for 6BR (White House), this is very competitive for a Superhost property near the action.
