## Notes

- **Paros has one of the better nightlife scenes in the Cyclades** outside of Mykonos — Naoussa in particular has a young, lively bar strip (Linnaeus Street) that runs late in summer. More affordable than Mykonos.
- Paros is a beautiful island — golden beaches, traditional villages, Cycladic architecture. A better balance of scenery and nightlife than purely scenic islands.
- Only one listing qualifies with 5BR/7 beds within budget. The 4.71★ rating and 28 reviews means it needs review-checking before booking.
- Flights to Paros: **no direct UK flights** — ferry from Athens (Piraeus, ~5–6 hrs) or short domestic flight from Athens. This adds a travel day each way.
- June: warm (~27°C), windy (the Meltemi starts in late June — Paros can be quite breezy).
