## Notes

- **Ayia Napa is one of Europe's premier party destinations** — the club scene rivals Ibiza, especially in June/July/August.
- Nissi Beach is the iconic beach party location; walkable or a very short drive from most Ayia Napa villas.
- Cyprus has excellent flight connections from the UK, with both Larnaca (LCA) and Paphos (PFO) airports served. Ayia Napa is ~40 min from Larnaca.
- **June is ideal** — peak season, warm seas, full club schedule open.
- Protaras is quieter than Ayia Napa but still has nightlife; if the main party is Ayia Napa Town/Nissi Beach, prefer villas within Ayia Napa rather than Protaras.
- The Anemelia 7BR at £2,870 for 4 nights represents strong value for 7 guests (~£103/person/night).
